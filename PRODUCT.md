# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Primary (Dashboard):** EMS Doctors who need to clock in/out, check history, manage the OP queue, calculate weekly bonuses, and access administrative settings.
- **Primary (Homepage):** General citizens and server players looking for hospital announcements, active recruitment info, public rules/regulations, treatment fee lists, and blacklist verification.

## Product Purpose
The official portal and shift management system for the EMS Central Hospital in FiveM. It functions as a public informational and recruitment hub for players in the city, while providing a secure operational dashboard for authorized medical staff.

## Brand Personality
Modern (ทันสมัย), Easy to understand (เข้าใจง่าย), Not cluttered (ไม่รก), and clean glassmorphic dark-medical-themed.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered grids with dense, competing widgets.
- Flat, uninspired gray SaaS dashboards.
- Overly bouncy animations that degrade performance.
- AI tells like em-dashes, uppercase text kickers, and generic SaaS hero-metrics.

## Design Principles
1. **Clear Split Focus:** Maintain a clean separation between public city resources (news, rules, fees, recruitment) and private staff operational gateways.
2. **Frictionless Entry:** Keep the staff login route fast, direct, and distraction-free for doctors entering/exiting their shifts.
3. **Readability & Hierarchy:** Design with spacious layouts, clear type contrasts, high text contrast, and no unnecessary borders or lines.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast text (minimum 4.5:1 ratio) to ensure ease of reading under varying dark ambient settings.
- Suppress animations for `prefers-reduced-motion` using instant transitions or simple crossfades.
- Fully responsive layout ensuring that citizens can read rules and fees comfortably on mobile devices.
